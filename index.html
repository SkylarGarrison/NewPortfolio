Welcome to My Page!


My name is Skylar Garrison and I am a budding software developer from the birthplace of Rock and Roll - Cleveland, Ohio.

Growing up, I wanted to be many things, but my top two choices were to be a teacher or an engineer. What type of teacher or engineer I wanted to be changed with my experiences, but I had three core wants for my career.

  1. The ability to learn and face new challenges every day
  2. To be able to work and interact with others
  3. To make people happy!

About Me

I started my coding adventure in highschool, with a web design and game development class. Though I've explored other interests, software development has always felt like home to me. No matter what, coding is always where I feel my happiness, and I'm excited to fully explore a career in this field.

One of my favorite things about software development is that it usually requires a team effort, rather than being a solo career. I'm at my best when I'm working with other people and exhanging thoughts and ideas for the best outcome possible.

When I'm not coding or working on projects, I enjoy spending time outdoors or relaxing at home. I love walks and bike rides, as well as reading, listening to music, and cooking for my family.
