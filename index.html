About Me

My love for web development began in 10th grade, when I took a video game and web design course. After graduating, I tried exploring different fields, but everything pointed back to coding. I'm at my happiest when I'm completing difficult coding challenges.

One of my favorite things about this field is the ability to collaborate with other developers. Whether it be coming together to perform a common goal, or sharing ideas for individual projects, there's something great about sharing a community with other like-minded individuals.
